## 🧭 How to Use These Resources

1. **Skim first** — don't read end-to-end. Find the section that answers your immediate question.
2. **Take notes in your own words** — copy-pasting doesn't build understanding.
3. **Cite everything** — at the bottom of your `REPORT.md`, list every link you used. Plagiarism (even unintentional) is a fail in industry.
4. **Pair theory with practice** — for every concept you read, write a small code snippet to verify you understand it.
